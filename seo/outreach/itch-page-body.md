🌐 **Meccha Chameleon Art** is a fan-made browser companion for Meccha Chameleon, the paint-based hide-and-seek Steam game by LEMORION.

## What's inside

- **50 hiding spots** across 5 hand-picked maps:
  - Vintage Room (introductory)
  - Cow Farm (rural)
  - Brick Tavern (mid-game)
  - Grand Ballroom (advanced)
  - Blue Parlor (expert)
- **Color analysis**: each spot has RGB/hex paint recommendations
- **Difficulty rating** (🟢/🟡/🔴) and **seeker counter-tips**
- **Bilingual** (English + 中文)
- **Mobile-friendly** — works in any browser, no download

## Why this exists

I'm a fan who got tired of alt-tabbing to find camo spots. This is the visual atlas I wish existed when I started playing.

## Tech

- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Static export (fast loads, no server logic)
- Open source: https://github.com/zlc000190/AwesomeMecchaChameleonHideSpot
