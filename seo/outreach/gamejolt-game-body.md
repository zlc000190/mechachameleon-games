🌐 Fan-made browser companion for the Steam hide-and-seek game Meccha Chameleon by LEMORION.

50 hiding spots across 5 maps (Vintage Room / Cow Farm / Brick Tavern / Grand Ballroom / Blue Parlor), each with:
- Difficulty rating
- Best paint colors (RGB/hex)
- Seeker counter-tip
- Screenshot
